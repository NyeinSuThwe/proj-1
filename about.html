About My Melody

My Melody is a white rabbit born in the Forest of Mariland on January 18th. She is known for her kindness, thoughtfulness, and love of baking. Her most treasured possession is her pink hood, a gift made by her grandmother.

Although small and simple in appearance, she represents friendship, warmth, and the joy found in everyday activities like sharing food and spending time with loved ones.

Character Profile

  • Birthday: January 18th
  • Birthplace: Forest of Mariland
  • Favorite Food: Almond Pound Cake
  • Hobbies: Baking & Reading
  • Special Item: Pink Hood from Grandma
  • Best Friend: Flat the Mouse

What makes My Melody special is her ability to bring out the best in those around her. She reminds us that even the smallest gestures of kindness can make the world brighter.
