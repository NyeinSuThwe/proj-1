About My Melody

My Melody is a charming white rabbit born in the Forest of Mariland on January 18th. She’s known for her gentle personality, love of baking, and her treasured pink hood that never leaves her side. Her favorite treat is almond pound cake, which she loves to share with friends.

Character Profile

  • Birthday: January 18th
  • Birthplace: Forest of Mariland
  • Favorite Food: Almond Pound Cake
  • Hobbies: Baking & Reading
  • Special Item: Pink Hood from Grandma
  • Best Friend: Flat the Mouse

What makes My Melody special is her caring nature and ability to bring out the best in everyone she meets. Her adventures highlight friendship, courage, and finding magic in everyday moments.
