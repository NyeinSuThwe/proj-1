About My Melody

My Melody is a white rabbit from the Forest of Mariland, known for her warm personality and love of baking. Her pink hood—made by her grandmother—is her most treasured item and a symbol of comfort.

This page shares a simple snapshot of who she is and why her stories feel cozy and kind. It’s written with plain HTML so the structure is easy to follow and accessible to everyone.

Character Profile

  • Birthday: January 18
  • Birthplace: Forest of Mariland
  • Favorite Treat: Almond pound cake
  • Hobbies: Baking & reading
  • Special Item: Pink hood from Grandma
  • Best Friend: Flat the Mouse

What makes My Melody special is how she brings out the best in others. Small acts of kindness, shared snacks, and quiet moments with friends are the heart of her world.
