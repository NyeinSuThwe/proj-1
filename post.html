Structure with a Smile: HTML-Only Pages Inspired by My Melody

When you strip away CSS and JavaScript, what remains is the pure content. This forces clarity in hierarchy, semantics, and accessibility.

Using headings, sections, and articles makes content flow naturally. Helpful alt text ensures everyone understands the visuals.

Even without styling, a clean structure makes the website readable and easy to navigate.
