Welcome to My Melody’s World

Welcome to the enchanting world of My Melody, Sanrio’s beloved white rabbit who has been spreading joy since 1975. With her signature pink hood crafted by her grandmother, My Melody represents kindness, friendship, and the simple pleasures of life in the magical Forest of Mariland.

Join us as we explore her world: heartwarming adventures, cozy baking sessions, and friendships that teach meaningful lessons about compassion and community.
